*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 F401RE. The aim of this demo is to explain
how PAL works in ChibiOS.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "Hello ChibiOS".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2015/02/10/hello-chibios/
