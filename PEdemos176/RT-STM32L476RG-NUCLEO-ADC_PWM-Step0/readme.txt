*** About ***
This demo runs on STM32 Nucleo-64 L476RG. The aim of this demo is to explain
how ADC and PWM works in ChibiOS.

*** Related article ***
For more information read the article "Sampling and Dimming".
http://www.playembedded.org/blog/en/2015/02/16/sampling-and-dimming/
